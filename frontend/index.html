Does ChatGPT mention my brand?

Most brands are completely invisible to AI engines and have no idea. Dablin's AI Visibility Check generates 7 targeted queries across 3 buyer intent stages and sends them to ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude simultaneously. You get a full table showing which AI engines mention your brand per query, which competitors appear instead, and the full AI response for every row. The 3 query types cover the full buyer journey: problem queries reach buyers before they know your category exists, category queries target buyers who know what they need, and comparison queries catch decision-stage buyers who are ready to switch.

What is the AI Visibility Audit?

The AI Visibility Audit runs 12 technical checks to find exactly why AI engines cannot find or understand your pages. What are the Google March 2026 ranking signals?

Google's March 2026 core update introduced two new ranking signals that most SEO tools do not check yet. Information Gain evaluates whether your page adds something original to the internet — unique data, author signals, structured content, or first-hand experience. Pages that are generic summaries of existing content are penalised. AI Overview eligibility checks whether your page has FAQPage or HowTo schema, or question-structured H2 and H3 headings, which increases the chance of appearing in Google's AI-generated search summaries. Dablin checks both signals in the SEO Audit.
